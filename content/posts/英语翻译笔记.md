【五】A组核心短语

1. 时间与背景
   · In recent years (近年来)
   · for a long time (持续很长时间，常与现在完成时连用)
   · for a while (一段时间)
   · in the long run (长远来看)
2. 地点与方位
   · be located in / on / at (坐落于)
   · across China (中国各地)
   · along the bank of (沿岸)
   · from the bottom up (自下而上)
3. 因果与目的
   · owing to / due to (由于)
   · therefore / hence (因此)
   · enable … to … (使…能够…)
   · take measures to (采取措施做某事)
   · take action (采取行动)
4. 描述与引出
   · according to / based on (根据)
   · It is recorded that (据记载)
   · as follows (如下)
   · among these / which / them (其中)
   · This is so-called (这就是所谓的)
   · can be traced back to (可以追溯到)
5. 重要概念表达
   · attach importance to (重视)
   · be satisfied with (满足于)
   · play an important role (发挥重要作用)
   · have an effect on (对…有影响)
   · account for … (percent/share) (占…（百分比/份额）)
   · hit new highs / reach a new high (创新高)
6. 固定搭配
   · living standard (生活水平)
   · a series of (一系列)
   · the variety of / a variety of (…的多样性 / 各种各样的)
   · Silk Road (丝绸之路)
   · national treasure (国宝)
   · driving force (驱动力)
   · harvest season (收获季节)
   · work out (健身)
7. 动词短语
   · come into effect (生效)
   · prevent ... from (阻止…做某事)
   · be about to (即将)
   · be delayed due to / be late for (因…而晚)
   · long for (渴望)
   · be renowned as / for (以…身份/因…而出名)
8. 其他必备
   · no longer (不再)
   · People live harmoniously with nature. (人与自然和谐共生)
   · on the eve of (在…前夕)
   · This kind of (这种)
   · limited to (限制于)
   · upside down (颠倒)
